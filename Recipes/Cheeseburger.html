Cheeseburger


Ingredients:


  • 1 pound ground beef (80% lean, 20% fat for a juicier burger)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of your favorite cheese (cheddar, American, Swiss, etc.)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced red onions
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise (or your preferred condiments)

Instructions


  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop griddle over medium-high heat.
  2. While the grill is heating up, divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties. Season both sides of each patty with salt and pepper.
  3. Place the patties on the preheated grill or griddle. Cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side for medium doneness, or adjust the cooking time to your preference.
  4. In the last minute of cooking, add a slice of cheese to each patty, allowing it to melt.
  5. While the burgers are cooking, lightly toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  6. Assemble your cheeseburgers by placing the cooked patties with melted cheese on the bottom half of each bun.
  7. Top the burgers with lettuce, sliced tomatoes, red onions, and pickles if desired.
  8. Spread your favorite condiments on the top half of the buns, such as ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise.
  9. Place the top half of the bun over the toppings to complete the burger.
  10. Serve your delicious cheeseburgers hot, and enjoy!
